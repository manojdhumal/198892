About Us

Our Short Story

Eyes On detective agency started its operations in August 2018. We are passionate about unearthing the truth and help people and private firms with their issues that need a secret investigation. Our investigation services are top class as we have a good mix of ex-corporate professionals and personnel that are well trained for the investigation job. Desiring to be a top private detective agency of India, our policies and work methods are structured and follow a standard operating procedure.

A competent team:

Our entire team of male and female detectives has received specialized training from retired and well known detective agents from the police department and armed forces. The training received from these professionals is the backbone of our detective agency and lends fuel to fire our passion to higher levels. The training imparted to our young detectives has made them daring, disciplined and fit; physically and mentally. Another big advantage that our team has is the knowledge of Civil and Criminal Laws which was imparted to them by the trainers. With our team and the professional approach, we have no doubt that Eyes On will soon be a top private detective agency of India. .

Latest surveillance devices:

Our competent team is ably supported by the most modern surveillance devices. We do not compromise with our investigations in any manner. Following the advice of the professional detectives, we have with us all the needed devices for investigation and the technical knowhow on how to use these devices to their best effect. We give our best at all the levels to deliver results as per your expectations.
