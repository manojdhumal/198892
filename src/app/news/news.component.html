EMPLOYEE BACKGROUND VERIFICATION:

Employees and employers trust each other and a work culture thus created takes both of them ahead in their respective goals. Getting information about the employer is very easy as everything is open to be shared to the people. You can go to the company website and check with past employees. Background checks about the recruiting company are easily accessible via ex-employees or even current employees of the organization through linked in like tools.

When it comes to verifying the credentials of an employee who has no past experience of working anywhere, it is a risky matter. How do you verify that the person going to enter your organization is ethical, trustworthy and reliable! No one can be sure about a fresher. Passing the interview is a cake walk as the employee comes well prepared or trained with the questions that are going to be posed during the interview. With hi-tech scanners, printers and copiers, one can get certificates and mark sheets that look just like originals. So if such a fraud candidate passes the interview, in majority cases, only the qualification certificates are checked casually. How many companies have the policy and tools of verifying the original documents? What about the character of the person? Does anyone check that?

Majority of the organizations do not have any system to verify whether the candidate who has passed the interview has actually studied in the institutes or passed the exams as claimed in the profile. Verification of the character of the person which is very vital; is never done. So, a fraud person getting in the organization is not unlikely at all!
